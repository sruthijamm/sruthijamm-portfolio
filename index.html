About me

Hi, I'm Sruthi. I work as a Software Developer at TCS. I have hands-on experience in data engineering and business analytics. I enjoy creating dependable data pipelines and turning messy data into insights that influence real business decisions.

My journey into data wasn’t planned. I initially pursued software engineering, but during my internships, I found that my true interest lay in data and AI.

Currently, I’m working to transition into a Data Scientist and AI Engineer role. I’m genuinely excited about the future of AI, especially agentic AI and how intelligent systems can change how businesses operate. I’m always learning and building, and I’m open to freelance projects that challenge me.

Outside of tech, I'm a photographer. You can see my work at shotbysruthi.com! It's a creative passion I'm gradually developing into something larger.
